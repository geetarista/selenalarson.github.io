--- layout: post title: "How To Become A Magic Rec On Twitter" date: 2014-07-21 categories: tech --- Ever wondered how Twitter decides what topics to tell you people are tweeting about? You know, those push notifications you get that say "two people you only sort of know via 140 character bursts are talking about something you care about." Or the @MagicRecs message you get if you're following the bot.

Well, turns out its algorithm isn't very smart, and, just by getting a group together and tweeting random stuff of zero importance, you, too, can become a Twitter recommendation.

Here's my friend Alex, who, as you can tell, really likes these push notifications.

If Twitter tells me that X number of people are talking about something there is a 100% chance I don't give a shit.
